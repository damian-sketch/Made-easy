Personal Insurances

Fire Insurance

Fire arises from various insured perils and causes damage to assets. The policy is suitable to cover business assets such as buildings, stocks in trade, work in progress, contents, plant and machinery. Fire is indiscriminate and devours anything in its path once it starts and hence the sum insured has to be appropriate and commensurate with the risk that the insured is exposed to

Motor Insurance

Get insurance for your private or commercial vehicle today. Ensuring that your car is well covered reduces the level of liability associated with vehicular accidents such as; Bodily injury liability, Property damage liability, Personal injury protection, Uninsured motorist, Underinsured motorist and Medical payments including a host of other likely liabilities.

House Insurance

Resolution Home Insurance Policy gives cover to compensate against losses or damage arising from any of the following causes: Fire, lightning, earthquakes, storms or floods, Explosion of domestic appliances, Riots, strikes and malicious damage, Damage caused by falling aircraft or other aerial devices, Bursting or overflowing of water tanks apparatus or pipes.
