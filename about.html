ABOUT MADE EASY INSURANCE AGENCY

Made Easy is a leading independent insurance broker catering for both businesses and private clients .Established in 1976 as an insurance agency, and later converting to an insurance Broker due to growing demand for its products .Made Easy insurance brokers Ltd is well placed to provide the excellent level of service you would expect from an Insurance Broker with over 35 years’ experience. Whatever be your insurance needs, we have the expertise, experience and resources to meet your requirements. For Corporate clients we arrange all types of commercial cover in every industry/ sector – from motor insurance to Professional Indemnity protection. Over the past few years, Made Easy Insurance broker has been continuously consolidating its position and is now renowned as one of the major players in the local market. We are vibrant, customer-focused organization and are driven by a passion for delivering highest standard market practice with simplicity & integrity to our clients and their needs.
